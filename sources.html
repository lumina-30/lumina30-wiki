Provenance and limits

Primary source policy

This PoC summarizes only claims traceable to official public LUMINA-30 materials. It is a map, not a new authority.

Selection rule

Sources were selected for direct relevance to the ten concepts, public accessibility, and an official lumina-30 organization or lumina-30.github.io location. Search results and this Wiki are discovery aids, not proof of adoption, consensus, certification, legality, or safety.

Handling uncertainty

Where official public materials do not establish a universal threshold, legal authority, technical design, or evidence standard, the concept pages say so. This PoC does not fill those gaps by inference.
